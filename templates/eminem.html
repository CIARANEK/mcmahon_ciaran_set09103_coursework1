Eminem: Curtian Call
Curtain Call: The Hits is the first greatest hits compilation album by American rapper Eminem. It was released on December 6, 2005, under Aftermath Entertainment, Shady Records, and Interscope Records.
  • Artist: Eminem
  • Release Date: 2005
  • Label: Aftermath Records
  • Genres: Hip Hop Music, Gangsta Rap, Hardcore Hip Hop
