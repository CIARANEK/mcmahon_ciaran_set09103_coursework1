The Essential Michael Jackson

 The Essential Michael Jackson is a greatest hits compilation album by American singer Michael Jackson. It was released on July 19, 2005, by Sony Music's catalog division Legacy Recordings as part of The Essential series.
  • Artist: Michael Jackson
  • Release Date: 2005
  • Label: Epic/Sony Records
  • Genres: Pop Music, Rock Music, Soul Music, Rhythm And Blues, Funk, Pop Rock, New Jack Swing
