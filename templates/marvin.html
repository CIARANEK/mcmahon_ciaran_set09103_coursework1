Marvin Gaye's Greatest Hits

 Marvin Gaye's Greatest Hits is a compilation album released by American R&B/soul singer and Motown legend Marvin Gaye, released on the Motown label in 1976.
  • Artist: Marvin Gaye
  • Release Date: 1976
  • Label: Tamla
  • Genres: Soul Music, Rhythm And Blues
